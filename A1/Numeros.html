4. The "100" Dilemma

CIEN

Use EXACTLY for 100

  • Exact number 100.
  • Before a noun (100 books, 100 people).

✅ "Tengo cien dólares."

✅ "Tengo cien libros."

CIENTO...

Use for 101 to 199

  • When followed by another number.
  • Think of it as "One hundred AND..."

✅ "Ciento uno (101)."

✅ "Ciento cincuenta (150)."

Important!

Never say "Un cien" or "Un ciento". Just "Cien" or "Ciento".

5. The Gender Trap (200-900)

Unlike English, numbers from 200 to 900 behave like adjectives. They must match the gender of the object.

Masculine Objects

Ending: -OS

200 Gatos Doscientos
400 Libros Cuatrocientos
500 Pesos Quinientos

Feminine Objects

Ending: -AS

200 Casas Doscientas
400 Personas Cuatrocientas
500 Flores Quinientas

Spelling Changes!

Watch out for 500 (Quinientos), 700 (Setecientos), and 900 (Novecientos). They change their root spelling!
