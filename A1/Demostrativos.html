Los Demostrativos

Level A1 · Demonstrative adjectives & pronouns

Learn how to point at things in Spanish.

What are demonstratives?

Instruction: Read the concept below.

Demonstratives answer "Which one?" (¿Cuál?). In Spanish we choose the word by:

  • Distance — how far is it?
  • Gender — masculine or feminine
  • Number — singular or plural

The 3 zones of distance

Instruction: Memorise the three zones and their Spanish words.

HEREESTE / ESTAVery close · I can touch it
THEREESE / ESAMedium · Near the listener
OVER THEREAQUEL / AQUELLAFar from both

Zone 1: Near (cerca)

Instruction: Use for things you can touch. "Esto" is only for abstract ideas.

SingularPlural
Masc.Este (this)Estos (these)
Fem.Esta (this)Estas (these)

Zone 2: Medium (media)

Instruction: Use for things near the listener or a short distance away.

SingularPlural
Masc.Ese (that)Esos (those)
Fem.Esa (that)Esas (those)

Zone 3: Far (lejos)

Instruction: Use for things far away, often outdoors.

SingularPlural
Masc.AquelAquellos
Fem.AquellaAquellas
